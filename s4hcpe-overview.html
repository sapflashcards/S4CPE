The Clean Core concept is central to the RISE with SAP Methodology. It refers to keeping a customer's core system as clean as possible to avoid issues with upgrading software. This diagram shows the five dimensions of Clean Core (Business Processes, Extensibility, Data, Integration, and Operation), its key benefits, and the importance of strong governance across all technical dimensions.
